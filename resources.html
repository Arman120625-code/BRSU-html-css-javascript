Scholarships

BRSU is committed to helping Rohingya students find and apply for scholarships. Below are some scholarship opportunities specifically available for Rohingya students or those with refugee status in Bangladesh.

DAFI Scholarship Program

The DAFI (Albert Einstein German Academic Refugee Initiative) scholarship program offers refugees the possibility to pursue undergraduate studies in their country of asylum.

Eligibility: Recognized refugees with completed secondary education

Application Period: January-February annually

Learn more →

BRSU Education Fund

Our organization provides small grants to promising Rohingya students to help cover educational expenses, including books, supplies, and transportation.

Eligibility: Rohingya students enrolled in educational institutions in Bangladesh

Application Period: Quarterly reviews (March, June, September, December)

Apply now →

International Scholarships Database

We maintain a comprehensive database of international scholarships that accept applications from refugee students. Our team regularly updates this resource with new opportunities.
