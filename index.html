Our Mission

The Bangladesh Rohingya Student Union (BRSU) is dedicated to supporting Rohingya students in their educational journey, fostering community connections, and advocating for educational rights and opportunities. We strive to empower the next generation of Rohingya leaders through academic excellence, cultural preservation, and social engagement.
