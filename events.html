Football Tournament

Time: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM
Location: Kutupalong Registered Camp,Ukhiya,Cox'sbazar.

BRSU is proud to announce its upcoming Football Tournament, scheduled to take place from October 15th, 2025, at the Camp-12 Football Ground in Cox's Bazar. This exciting event will feature 8 teams representing different Rohingya camps, competing in a spirit of unity and sportsmanship. The tournament aims to bring youth together, build leadership skills, and strengthen community ties through the power of sport. Spectators can look forward to live commentary, engaging audience activities, and special appearances by respected community leaders. The winning team will be awarded trophies, medals, and educational gifts as a symbol of both athletic and academic encouragement.
