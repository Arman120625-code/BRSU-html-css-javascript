About BRSU

Learn about our organization, mission, and the team behind BRSU

Our Story

The Bangladesh Rohingya Student Union (BRSU) was established in 2020 by a group of dedicated Rohingya students who recognized the need for a unified platform to address the educational challenges faced by their community. What began as informal study groups in refugee camps has grown into a structured organization with a clear mission to empower Rohingya students through education, community building, and advocacy.

Today, BRSU serves hundreds of Rohingya students across Bangladesh, providing educational resources, mentorship programs, scholarship information, and community events. Our work is guided by the belief that education is a fundamental right and a powerful tool for positive change.

Our Mission

To support and empower Rohingya students in Bangladesh through educational resources, community building, and advocacy, enabling them to achieve academic excellence and become leaders in their communities.

Our Vision

A world where all Rohingya students have equal access to quality education, opportunities for personal and professional growth, and the ability to contribute positively to society while preserving their cultural identity.

Our Core Values

Education

We believe in the transformative power of education and are committed to making learning resources accessible to all Rohingya students.

Community

We foster a sense of belonging and mutual support among Rohingya students, creating spaces for connection and collaboration.

Advocacy

We amplify the voices of Rohingya students and advocate for policies that promote educational equity and inclusion.

Cultural Preservation

We honor and preserve Rohingya cultural heritage while embracing educational advancement and integration.

Empowerment

We empower Rohingya students to take ownership of their educational journeys and become agents of positive change.

Collaboration

We work collaboratively with educational institutions, NGOs, and community organizations to maximize our impact.
